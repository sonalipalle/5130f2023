
Web Services and Cloud Computing in Healthcare

Abstract:

In the dynamic landscape of healthcare, the integration of web services and cloud computing has ushered in a transformative era.  Emphasizing the pivotal contributions of web services to E-health systems, mobile medical records, and telemedicine services. Web services, with their robust data exchange capabilities, secure communication, and scalability, play a central role in reshaping the healthcare landscape. They underpin the efficient management of patient data, enabling seamless access to medical records, and facilitating the delivery of healthcare services, irrespective of geographical constraints. This analysis should showcase the real-world applications and display the pressing need to harness full potential of web services as the backbone of innovation in healthcare, offering a pathway to patient-centric, accessible, and quality medical services.
